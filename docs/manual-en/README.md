## 1. Sign In

Log in to your Vaulted account with email and password, and verify your identity with two-factor authentication.

### How to Use

1. Open the Vaulted app
2. Enter your email in the Email field
3. Enter your password in the Password field
4. Tap **Log in** to enter

If your account has two-factor authentication enabled, you will see the verification screen.

### Verify with MFA Code

1. Open your authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy, etc.)
2. Copy the 6-digit code displayed
3. Enter the code in the Vaulted app
4. Tap **Verify** to complete sign in

### Accept an Invitation

If you received an invitation by email:

1. Open the invitation link in your email
2. Create a strong password (minimum 12 characters, with uppercase, lowercase, number, and symbol)
3. Confirm the password by entering it again
4. Tap **Create account**

If your role requires MFA, you will set up the code in the next step.

### Tips

- If you forgot your password, contact your family administrator
- The MFA code changes every 30 seconds — if it expires, wait for the next one
- Save the invitation link in a safe place; it expires in 48 hours

## 2. Dashboard

The main screen where you see a summary of your inventory, properties, and quick access to the most used features.

### How to Use

1. When you open the app, you are on the Dashboard
2. Below the greeting, you will see two cards: **Total Value** and **Total Items**
3. Below that, you will see the status of your items (how many are active, on loan, in repair, etc.)

### View Your Properties

1. Scroll down to see all your properties
2. Each card shows: name, location, type, and item count
3. Tap any property to view its floors and rooms

### Use Quick Actions

In the **QUICK ACTIONS** section, you have 4 buttons:

1. **Scan QR** — opens the camera to scan an item QR code
2. **AI Assistant** — opens the AI chat
3. **Operations** — view active loans and movements
4. **Maintenance** — check scheduled maintenance

### Open the User Menu

1. Tap the circle with your initials or avatar in the top right corner
2. From there you can:
   - Go to Maintenance
   - Go to Settings
   - Sign out

### Tips

- Pull down to refresh the data
- If you are an Owner or Manager, you will see the **Add** button to add properties
- Total values are only visible to Owners and Auditors

## 3. Properties

Manage your properties, floors, and rooms. Each property can be your primary home, vacation home, or rental.

### How to Use

### View a Property

1. From the Dashboard, tap a property
2. You will see the cover photo, address, and type
3. Scroll down to see floors and rooms

### Add a Property

1. On the Dashboard, tap **Add** next to **YOUR PROPERTIES**
2. Fill in: name, type, address
3. Optional: add a cover photo
4. Tap **Save** to create

### Add a Floor

1. Inside a property, tap the purple floating button
2. Select **Add floor**
3. Enter the floor name (e.g., First Floor, Second Floor)
4. Tap **Add floor**

### Add a Room

1. On a floor, tap the **+** next to the floor name
2. Enter the room name
3. Select the room type
4. Tap **Add room**

### Add Sections

A room can have sections (drawers, shelves, cabinets):

1. Inside a room, tap **Sections**
2. To add: tap the + icon or scan with AI
3. Define code (e.g., 1A), name, and section type
4. Tap **Add section**

### Change Cover Photo

1. Inside a property, tap the camera icon
2. Choose camera or gallery
3. Take or select the photo
4. The photo will upload automatically

### Tips

- Property type affects the badge color (Primary=gold, Vacation=blue, Rental=gray)
- **Unlocated** items appear in an orange banner — tap **Assign** to locate them
- Only Owners and Managers can add or edit properties

## 4. Inventory

Browse, search, and manage all your inventoried items in one place.

### How to Use

### Search Items

1. From the Dashboard, tap **Scan QR** or go to **Asset Directory**
2. In the search bar, enter name, tag, or serial number
3. Results appear automatically

### Filter by Category

1. Tap a category: All, Furniture, Art, Technology, Wardrobe, Vehicles, Wine, Sports
2. The list updates to show only that category

### Filter by Status

1. Tap a status: Active, Loaned, Repair, Storage
2. You can combine category and status filters

### Filter by Property

1. Tap a property name
2. You will only see items from that property

### Sort Results

1. Tap **Recent** to see the most recent
2. Tap **Value** to see by value (Owners/Auditors only)
3. Tap **Name A-Z** to sort alphabetically

### View Unlocated Items

1. Tap **Unlocated** to see items without floor or room assigned

### View Item Details

1. Tap any item
2. You will see: photo, value, specifications, history, maintenance
3. If you can edit, tap **Edit Item** at the bottom

### Tips

- Use multiple filters to find specific items quickly
- The **Unlocated** filter is useful for assigning pending items
- Total values are only visible to Owners and Auditors

## 6. QR Scanning

Use the camera to scan QR codes and quickly find items or rooms.

### How to Use

### Scan an Item

1. From the Dashboard, tap **Scan QR**
2. Point the camera at the item QR code
3. The code must be inside the white frames
4. When detected, you will automatically go to the item detail

### Scan a Room

1. Point at a room section QR code
2. You will go directly to that section in the location screen

### Tips

- Keep the QR code well-lit
- Hold the phone steady (without movement)
- The code must fit within the 4 white frames
- Item QR codes have format: `vaulted://items/ID`
- Room section QR codes have format: `vaulted://rooms/ID?section=CODE`
- If the camera does not detect, clean the lens and improve lighting

## 7. Movements / Operations

Manage loans, repairs, and disposals of items. Track where your items are at all times.

### How to Use

### View Operations

1. From the Dashboard, tap **Operations** or go to the tab
2. You will see two tabs: Active and History
3. Active = operations in progress
4. History = completed operations

### Create a New Operation

1. Tap the **New Operation** button (+)
2. Select the type:
   - **Loan** — lend items to someone
   - **Repair** — send for repair
   - **Disposal** — decommission/remove
3. Define a title for the operation
4. Tap **Create**

### Scan Items

1. Tap an active operation
2. Point at each item QR code
3. Each scan adds the item to the operation
4. You will see the counter increase

### Return Items (Check-in)

1. In an active operation, tap **Check-in**
2. Scan the items being returned
3. Items return to their previous location

### Complete Operation

1. In an active operation, tap the menu (three dots)
2. Tap **Mark as complete**
3. Confirm — items not returned will be marked as **MISSING**

### Cancel Operation

1. Tap the menu (three dots)
2. Tap **Cancel operation**
3. Confirm to cancel

### Tips

- If you close the app during an operation, you can continue later from **Draft in progress**
- Items on loan show status **Loaned**
- Only Owners and Managers can create operations

## 8. Maintenance

Schedule and track maintenance of your valuable items.

### How to Use

### View Maintenance

1. From the Dashboard, tap **Maintenance**
2. You will see 4 tabs:
   - **Overdue** — overdue tasks
   - **This Week** — tasks due this week
   - **Upcoming** — future tasks
   - **Completed** — completed tasks

### Add Maintenance

From the item detail:

1. Go to the item
2. Scroll to **Maintenance**
3. Tap **Add maintenance**
4. Define: title, date, recurrence (optional)
5. Tap **Save**

### Complete Maintenance

1. In the maintenance list, tap the item
2. Tap the **Complete** button
3. It is marked as completed

### View Details

1. Tap any task
2. You will see: title, date, recurrence, status
3. AI suggests tasks with risk percentage

### Tips

- Overdue tasks appear in red
- Tasks due this week appear in gold
- Enable recurrence for repetitive tasks (e.g., annual jewelry cleaning)
- AI can suggest maintenance based on item type

## 9. Insurance

Manage your insurance policies and protect your valuable items.

### How to Use

### View Policies

1. From the bottom bar, tap the shield icon
2. List of all your insurance policies
3. Each card shows: provider, number, coverage, expiry, insured items

### Add a Policy

1. Tap the + button at the top
2. Enter: provider, policy number, coverage type
3. Define: coverage amount, premium, start and expiration dates
4. Tap **Save**

### View Policy Details

1. Tap any policy
2. You will see: total coverage, premium, dates, insured items
3. From here you can edit or add items

### Add Insured Items

1. In the policy, tap the + button in **Insured Items**
2. Search and select items
3. Tap **Attach**

### Gap Analysis (pro beta)

1. In a policy, tap **Gap Analysis**
2. AI analyzes which items are uninsured
3. You will see gaps in your coverage

### Delete Policy

1. In details, tap the Trash icon
2. Confirm to delete

### Tips

- Policies expiring soon appear in red
- Coverage amount is what the insurance pays in case of total loss
- Insure high-value items first
- A gap greater than 0 means the item exceeds the policy coverage
- **Fully Uninsured** = item is not on any policy

### How to Use

### View Your Wardrobe

2. You will see your Wardrobe category items in a grid

### Filter by Type

### Filter by Cleaning

1. In Outfits, tap **Create Outfit**

## 11. AI Scanning

Use artificial intelligence to add items quickly. Take photos of the item and receipt, and AI automatically extracts the information.

### How to Use

### Start Scanning

1. From a property, tap the purple floating button
2. Select **AI Scan**
3. Or from the Dashboard, go to AI Scan

### Step 1: Item Photo

1. Point at the item and tap the camera button
2. Or tap the gallery icon to select
3. AI identifies the item

### Step 2: Receipt Photo (Optional)

1. Tap to capture the receipt
2. Or select from gallery
3. Or tap **Skip** to omit

### AI Analyzes

1. You will see **Analyzing...**
2. AI extracts: name, category, value, serial

### Review and Save

1. Review the extracted information
2. Edit as needed
3. Select the location (floor/room)
4. Tap **Save** to add

### Tips

- The receipt is optional but helps get exact value
- AI suggests category and subcategory
- Always review information before saving

## 12. AI Assistant

Chat with artificial intelligence about your inventory. Ask questions in natural language and get useful answers.

### How to Use

### Start Conversation

1. From the Dashboard, tap **AI Assistant**
2. Or from anywhere, find the shortcut

### Ask Questions

Type in the field below and tap send. Examples:

- Where is my Rolex?
- What items do I have on loan?
- List all items worth more than $10,000
- Move my living room furniture
- What jewelry do I have that is most valuable?

### View Suggestions

In the initial state, you will see suggested questions. Tap any to send automatically.

### Clear Conversation

1. Tap the Trash icon at the top
2. Confirm to clear the history

### Tips

- Be specific for better results
- You can ask for actions (move, list, etc.)
- Responses include links to items
- Total values are only visible to Owners and Auditors

## 13. Team / Users

Manage members of your family or team. Invite new users and assign roles.

### How to Use

### View Members

1. Go to Settings > Team members
2. Or from the Dashboard, tap your avatar > Team
3. List of all members with their role and status

### Available Roles

- **Owner** — full access, can invite
- **Manager** — can manage properties and invite
- **Staff** — can add and edit items
- **Auditor** — can only view values and reports
- **Guest** — limited access

### Invite Someone

1. Tap the + invite button
2. Enter the person email
3. Select the role
4. Tap **Send invitation**
5. The person will receive an email with the link

### View Member Details

1. Tap any member
2. You will see: email, role, status, last access
3. Only Owners and Managers can view details

### Change Role

From User Detail (Owners only):

1. Tap the current role
2. Select the new role
3. Confirm

### Tips

- Only Owners can invite and change roles
- Pending members will show **Pending**
- The green circle indicates user currently online
